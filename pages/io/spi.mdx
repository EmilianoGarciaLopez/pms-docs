# SPI-interfaced IO module

The SPI-interfaced varient PMC-IO-SGP uses the [MCP23S17](https://ww1.microchip.com/downloads/en/DeviceDoc/20001952C.pdf) SPI port expander chip. The relevant register tables are found on pages 16 and 17 (sections 3.4-3.5).
